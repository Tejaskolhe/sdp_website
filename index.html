What is Grain Analyser?

We present a robust, precise, efficient, and lucrative method for Breed and Grade Classification of Wheat and Rice Grains. Grain Analyser is a AI Application to Determine Breed and Grade of Wheat / Rice Sample. It tells us about the quality and breed of grain.

Why Grain Analyser?

Qualitative grading and breed identification of Wheat is a challenging and important task. The manual process of grading the wheat includes segregation of the grains based on color, size, texture, and other physical parameters. Also, some of the aspects of the manual grading involve chemical treatment of the sample. This is a tedious and expensive process that requires human perception and judgment which is not reliable and is predisposed to manual biases. This necessitates an automated process that is inexpensive, efficient, and accurate.

How to use Grain Analyser?

  1. Click a photo of your produce.
  2. Upload it.
  3. Wait for some time and see the Result.
